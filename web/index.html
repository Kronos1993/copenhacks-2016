Create your GPG keys

GPG Keychain is an open source application that allows you to manage your OpenPGP keys. Create and modify your keys to encrypt and protect your data like emails or chats.

Go to GPG page

Give your GPG public key to Facebook

Go to your Facebook profile page and then in the "About" tab. Under the "Contact and basic info" you will find the field for the public key.

Go to Facebook

Install Messenger Privacy Guard plugin

The plugin will encrypt your messages between you and your friends on Facebook Messanger. On this way, no one expcept you and your receiver with public GPG key will know what are you talking about.
